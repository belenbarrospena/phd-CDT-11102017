Automated Teller Machines (ATM)

  • Hard to get to it
  • Hard to read the screen
  • Hard to remember your PIN
  • Instructions operate too quickly
  • Easy to forget your cash
  • Anxiety about security

Chip & PIN cards

  • Hard to insert card in POS device
  • Hard to recall your PIN
  • Hard to tell debit from credit cards
  • Hard to read the numbers on the card
  • Anxiety about feeling rushed

Telephone banking

  • Hard to recall passwords, codes, security questions
  • Hard to navigate long automated menus
  • Hard to process aural-only information
  • Hearing difficulties
  • Hard to authorise someone to do it for you

Online banking

  • No experience using ICTs
  • Lack of confidence
  • Fear over security
  • Dreadful interface usability & accessibility
  • Inconsistency (e.g. login procedures)
  • Lack of paper trail
